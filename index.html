Hi, I'm Rattakorn.

For over a decade, since 2013, I've been a physical therapist at Bangkok Phuket Hospital. My main passion and specialty is neurological rehabilitation, and I currently have the privilege of leading the Neurological Physiotherapist Team as the Incharge.

I also serve as Head of the Physical Therapy Department.

My philosophy is simple: treatment is a partnership. I believe the most effective rehabilitation comes from clear communication—I'm a big fan of public speaking and presenting clear health plans! I focus on being a good listener and a strong team player, both with my colleagues and my patients.

When I'm not at the hospital, I've had some incredible opportunities, like serving as the Physical Therapist for the Thai National Swim Team during the 32nd SEA Games in Cambodia. I'm a fast learner, active, and always looking for creative ways to help people.
